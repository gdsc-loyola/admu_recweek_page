A GLOBAL COMMUNITY

Communities empowered
through tech, by tech.

Developer Student Clubs (DSC) Loyola is a student-led tech organization inside the Ateneo De Manila University. The Katipunan chapter is one amongst the many Google Developer-powered communities that empower its members to *develop for change*. With tech education and altruistic innovation as its founding pillars, DSC equips its student-developers with the resources they need to create human-centered solutions for social impact. The organization aims to humanize the way society perceives technology by providing pathways for communities to thrive through DSC's innovations.

DSC Loyola empowers more than just its student memberbase. DSC takes pride in sparking change beyond its home turf by encouraging purposeful innovation and peer-to-peer collaboration inside and outside the walls of the organization.
